OSC Sequencer.

Lekture is an application designed to control applications or devices through OSC protocol. Send all the commands you want to all devices/applications you have. It give you a fine-grain control over your needs

Work in Progress

The development of this software has just been began in Dec. 2015. So it's quite young and should not be used in Production before its first release, which is planned sometime in 2016. The first release will be limited in term of features, but we want to increment each new feature when the previous one will be fully functionnal and bug-free.
